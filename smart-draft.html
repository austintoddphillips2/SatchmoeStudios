Blind bidding

Every round, all players submit a secret bid on the player they want — nobody sees anyone else's number until bids are locked in. Read the room, or get read.

You're working with a fixed budget for the whole draft. Overspend early chasing a star, and you'll be scraping for value the rest of the way.
